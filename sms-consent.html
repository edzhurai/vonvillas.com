SMS Consent & Opt-In Methods

Von Villas Voice Studio · Last updated: July 19, 2026

Von Villas Voice Studio offers two ways for students and their parents/guardians to opt in to receive SMS text messages from the studio. This page documents both, for transparency and for review purposes.

1. Verbal Opt-In (during enrollment)

When a student (or parent/guardian) enrolls for lessons, in person or by phone, studio staff read the following script aloud and record the end user's verbal response before adding their phone number to our SMS system:

Von Villas Voice Studio staff:

"As part of enrolling with Von Villas Voice Studio, we'd like to send you SMS reminders about your lesson schedule, cancellations, and important studio updates. Message frequency varies depending on your lesson schedule. Message and data rates may apply, depending on your mobile phone service plan. At any time you can get more help by replying HELP to these texts, or you can opt out completely by replying STOP. Terms and Conditions are available at https://vonvillas.com/terms.html and our Privacy Policy can be found at https://vonvillas.com/privacy.html. Is that okay with you?"

End user: "Yes" (verbal confirmation)

Von Villas Voice Studio staff: "Great — we'll send you a text confirming your enrollment shortly."

Only after receiving explicit verbal "yes" consent is the end user's phone number added to our SMS system.

2. Text-to-Opt-In (Keyword)

End users can also opt in (or re-subscribe after previously opting out) at any time by texting START to (571) 351-2223. Upon receiving START, an automatic confirmation message is sent:

"Von Villas Voice Studio: You are now opted-in to receive lesson reminders and studio updates. For help, reply HELP or text (571) 351-2223. To opt-out, reply STOP. Msg&Data rates may apply."

Opting Out

End users may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP or INFO for assistance.

More Information

See our Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policy for full program details, including message frequency, rates disclosures, and our commitment that mobile opt-in data is never shared with third parties for marketing purposes.
