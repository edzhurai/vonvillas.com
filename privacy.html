VV Studio — Privacy Policy (SMS)

VV Studio collects your mobile phone number solely to send lesson reminders, schedule updates, and occasional billing reminders.

We do not sell, rent, or share your mobile number with third parties for marketing purposes.

Message frequency varies based on your lesson schedule (typically 2–4 messages per week). Message and data rates may apply.

To opt out at any time, reply STOP. For help, reply HELP or contact us directly.
