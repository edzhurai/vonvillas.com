SMS Notification Program

Von Villas Voice Studio — Falls Church, VA — (703) 209-9779

Von Villas Voice Studio sends SMS text messages to students and parents who have provided their phone number and consented to receive notifications.

What we send

  • Lesson reminders (typically the day before a scheduled lesson)
  • Lesson cancellation notices
  • Schedule change notifications
  • Occasional studio announcements

Message frequency is typically 1–3 messages per week depending on your lesson schedule.

How to opt in

Students and parents opt in by providing a mobile phone number during enrollment and confirming they wish to receive SMS notifications from Von Villas Voice Studio. You may also contact us directly at (703) 209-9779 or vonvstudio@gmail.com to request SMS notifications.

How to opt out

To stop receiving SMS messages at any time, reply STOP to any message we send. You will receive one confirmation message and no further messages will be sent.

To resume notifications, reply START or contact us directly.

Help

Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or contact us at (703) 209-9779.

Costs

Von Villas Voice Studio does not charge for SMS notifications. Standard message and data rates from your mobile carrier may apply.

Privacy

Your phone number is used solely for studio communication and is never shared with or sold to third parties.
